Who Am I?

From a Macro Perspective, I am:

  • A grounded futurist: I believe progress comes from building on the tools and breakthroughs passed down to us, then applying them to real problems in the world. I see technology as one of the clearest paths to making businesses more efficient, people more capable, and communities more connected. For me, things like voice AI are not just trends, they are signs of where the future is going: simpler access, faster service, and smarter systems that help ordinary businesses compete at a higher level.
  • A salesman in an engineer’s body: I sit in an interesting space between software and people. My technical background gives me credibility, but my real edge is that I can speak to non technical people. I understand technical systems, but I also enjoy sales, client conversations, onboarding, cold calling, and explaining value to business owners in simple terms.
  • An instinctive opportunist: I like to believe my path has been carefully planned, but the truth is that many of my best moves have come from spotting an opening and acting on it quickly. I am not someone who waits until every detail is perfect. I move first, learn fast, and figure out the rest along the way.
